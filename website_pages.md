# Purpose

This document defines every website page except the Homepage.

Each page exists to answer a specific business question, build trust, and guide visitors toward becoming long-term clients.

Every page should support Nexora's positioning as a **Business Growth Partner**, not a traditional agency.

# Shared Rules

Every page must:

✓ Answer one primary business question.

✓ Reinforce Nexora's positioning as a Business Growth Partner.

✓ Focus on business outcomes before services.

✓ Follow the Design Language.

✓ Follow the Motion Language.

✓ End with a meaningful CTA.

✓ Link naturally to relevant pages.

✓ Maintain consistent spacing, typography, and interaction patterns.

Never:

✗ Repeat homepage content.

✗ Overload visitors with unnecessary information.

✗ Use generic agency messaging.

✗ Focus only on technical features.

✗ Add sections without a clear business purpose.

---

# Success Criteria

Every page should help visitors answer:

- What problem does Nexora solve?
- Why should I trust Nexora?
- Is this solution relevant to my business?
- What should I do next?

If these answers are not immediately clear, revise the page before implementation.
